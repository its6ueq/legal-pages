About PinIt - Notification Notes

PinIt is a simple and efficient note-taking app that keeps your important notes always visible in your notification panel. Perfect for quick reminders, shopping lists, and important information you need to access quickly.

Key Features:

  • Create and manage text notes easily
  • Pin important notes to your notification panel
  • Quick access to notes without opening the app
  • Dark and light theme support
  • Multiple language support (English, Vietnamese)
  • Local storage - your notes stay private on your device
  • No account required - start using immediately

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 4, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. PinIt - Notification Notes is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No personal data collection: We don't collect names, emails, or personal information
  • Local storage only: All your notes are stored locally on your device
  • No cloud sync: Your notes never leave your device
  • Anonymous analytics: Only anonymous usage data for app improvement
  • Ad-supported: Uses Google AdMob for advertisements (can be removed via purchase)
